About Me

Hometown

I was born and raised in the Twin Cities. Grew up in Minneapolis and now live in Saint Paul.

Why Coding?

Out of college I worked for a large medical software development company, Epic, providing technical support to hospitals across the country. This was first taste of coding. It was tangential to my core work of troubleshooting configuration issues with analysts, but I was curious about it. Most of the learning was on my own and difficult to fit into a day filled with meetings.

When I changed jobs after moving back to Minnesota I was again working alongside developers. I started to envy their position of fixing issues when they arose instead of simply flagging them as existing. Now 5 years into my professional work life I have decided to make a change and commit to learning to code through the University of Minnesota coding bootcamp.

Passion

I love and care deeply about the environment and the well-being of our natural habitat. I hope to pursue a long career in environmental technology. The vagueness of it excites me because it will challenge me to pursue many avenues of interest focused on the environment.
